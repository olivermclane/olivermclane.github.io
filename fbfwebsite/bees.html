Our Bees

At Farting Buddha Farms, we have had a couple different types of bees. This year we will be working with the Carniolans. Carniolan bees are consider the most gentle amoung the honey bee species. Carniolan bees are also know for being able to defend themselves aganist small pest that make their way into the hive. Another quality is that Carniolans have a remarkable ability to increase their workers population during the high nector periods. If you are looking to get into beekeeping the Carniolans are a great place to start
