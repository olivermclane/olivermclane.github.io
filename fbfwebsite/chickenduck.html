Our Chickens

At Farting Buddha Farms we have many different types of chickens. Including Cochins, Rhode Island Reds, Barred Rocks, Buff Orphingtons, Silver Laced Wyandotte, Plymouth Rock, and Ameraucanas. We have around twenty-five chickens in our Yard. We collected around a bakers dozen of eggs every day during the winter, and in the summer months, we usually get around eighteen eggs per day.

Do we sell eggs?

As we raise our new generation of hens, we are refraining from selling eggs to anyone who isn't local. If you have any questions feel free to contact us.
