What is Farting Buddha Farms?

We are a small Heber Valley homestead, with a small orchard, large garden, trip of goats (named Elsa, Macie, Charlotte, Olive, and Rowan), brace of ducks, brood of chickens, and a mess of Carniolan honey bees. Because we believe that the earth is a sacred place that sustains all life, we use only organic methods to grow our herbs and produce.

Honey Instructions

We hope you enjoy our raw honey. Our honey is poured directly from our hand-cranked spinner into sterile mason jars. Nothing is added to it, and is never heated to keep it clear. Good- quality honey that has not been super filtered to remove pollen, or cut with fructose corn syrup, crystallizes… and “yay!” it makes our raw honey easier to spread!

If you are bothered by the consistency, put the glass jar of honey in a pot of near – boiling water, never in a microwave, and let the crystals melt. Enjoy!
