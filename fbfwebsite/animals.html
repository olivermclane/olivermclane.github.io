What animals do we have?

At Farting Buddha Farms, we have mutliple different types of chickens, goats and we even have Peafowl. We have plans to add a pond this summer, in hopes of having koi and trout.

What do we harvest from the animals?

We collect farm fresh eggs from the chickens and ducks. During the springs and summer, we have milked our goats in the past, and made cheese and Half & Half

Do we sell our animals?

This happens to be our most asked question, we use to sell rabits and goats. But as of recent we no longer sell animals. For multiple reasons, in particular we have stop breeding our goats and collect our chickens eggs.
