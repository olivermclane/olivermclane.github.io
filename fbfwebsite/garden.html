Our Garden

Our Garden is located in a desert area covered in sage brush, and over the years, we have cleared out all of the sage brush and put up a large fence in hopes of keeping the deer out. We layed down landscaping fabric and then layed down gravel. We then put in our rasied beds on top of the gravel. In the middle we have a divider to keep the chickens out of the garden and to keep them in the orchard. Over the years we have begun to change the soil, we have begun to see increases in worms and other bugs crucial in process for growing food.
