Our Goats

We have quite a few goats on the farm, their names are Olive, Rowan, Macie, and Elsa. Olive is the most friendly of the bunch but all of them love people. Elsa is the mean goat and bullys all of the other goats. Rowan is our only boy and loves sitting in your lap. Finally, Macie is the most peaceful of all the goats, she loves just to sit and sunbath.
