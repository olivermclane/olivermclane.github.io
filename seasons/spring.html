Welcome to the Spring Information Page

The spring is my favorite time of the year, particularly because I live on a farm and we get to enjoy all the baby animals. In the spring, we see lots of rain mixed with the warmth of the summer. Typically everything begins to turn green and thrive during theses months. The snow from the winter months begins to melt and all the animals begin to come out of hibernation. We see all the babies from the breeding season, come into existance.
